Treatments

Implants

Dental implants are now becoming a more popular treatment than partial dentures and conventional bridges for the replacement of missing teeth. They provide a long term solution, slow down bone loss and preserve surrounding healthy tooth tissue.

Cosmetic Dentistry

We offer a wide range of treatment options to meet the individual needs of all our patients, whatever their age, budget and lifestyle.

Teeth Whitening

Cosmetic bleaching is becoming more and more popular and it's because of this that our dentists use the latest technology in the tooth whitening from Enlighten. Results last about three years and your teeth look lovely and iridescent.

Dentures

If you are experiencing any discomfort with your dentures call us to make an appointment with Les Wheatley, our denture specialist, and he will be happy to help and give you friendly advice.

Sleep Apnea

There are now clinically proven, effective solutions to this very common problem. Our dentists can provide you with a simple comfortable dental appliance for night-time wear.

Facial Rejuvenation

Achieve beautiful, smooth skin by removing moles and skin lesions painlessly, with minimal scarring. Choose cosmetic radio surgery with Dr Strawford's Avalon Aesthetics; here at Rock House Dental Practice.
